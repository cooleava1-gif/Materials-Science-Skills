# Civil / Construction Materials (General) Phrase Bank

Use this bank for general civil engineering materials, construction materials, and building material manuscripts not specifically covered by the WER-EA, cement-concrete, or durability phrase banks.

---

## phrase-bank

### material design and formulation

- `[Material A] was modified with [Material B] at dosages of [range] wt%[/phr/by weight of binder] to investigate the effect on [target property].`
- `The [mix design/formulation] was optimized based on [response variable] using [methodology: orthogonal experiment/response surface methodology/Taguchi design].`
- `A [control/baseline] specimen without [modifier/additive] was prepared under identical conditions for comparison.`

### mechanical performance

- `The [compressive/flexural/tensile/shear/bond] strength reached [value] [unit] at [dosage/condition], corresponding to a [percentage]% [increase/decrease] relative to the control.`
- `The [modulus of elasticity/stiffness/toughness] was determined from [test type] curves, showing a [trend] with [variable].`
- `Failure mode transitioned from [mode A] to [mode B] as [variable] increased, as observed by [visual/SEM/optical] inspection.`

### durability

- `After [number] [freeze-thaw/wet-dry/aging/UV exposure] cycles, the [property] retention was [value]%, [higher/lower] than the control group.`
- `[Moisture/water/chemical] resistance was evaluated by [test method], with [metric] of [value] indicating [improved/similar/poor] performance.`
- `The [degradation/deterioration] mechanism is attributed to [mechanism] based on [evidence: SEM/FTIR/mass loss/surface change].`

### testing and characterization

- `Test specimens were prepared according to [standard: ASTM/EN/GB/T/JTG] [number] with dimensions of [value] and cured at [temperature/humidity/duration].`
- `[Characterization method] was performed using [instrument model] at [parameters], with samples prepared by [method].`
- `Three replicates were tested for each condition, and results are reported as mean ± standard deviation.`

### modification and mechanism

- `The addition of [modifier] improved [property] through [mechanism], as evidenced by [characterization result].`
- `[FTIR/SEM/XRD/XPS/TGA/DSC] analysis reveals [structural/morphological/chemical] changes that correlate with the observed [performance] improvement.`
- `The optimum [modifier] content of [value]% balances the [target property] and [competing property] trade-off, beyond which [negative effect] dominates.`

### application context

- `[Material] is proposed for [application] where [performance requirement] is critical, though [limitation/concern] must be addressed before field adoption.`
- `Laboratory results suggest that [material] could be suitable for [application context], pending [field trial/scale-up/long-term validation].`
- `The [cost/sustainability/constructability] implications of using [material] include [benefit/challenge/factor].`

---

## reviewer-red-flags

- Reporting mechanical properties without standard test method citation.
- Claiming "optimum dosage" without showing the full dosage range including the descending branch.
- Durability claims based on short-term testing without explaining the acceleration factor.
- Comparing results with literature values from different test standards or conditions.
- Missing statistical analysis or error bars on reported properties.
- Overinterpreting small FTIR peak shifts without baseline correction or replicate spectra.

---

## safe-claim-patterns

- `Within the tested [variable] range, [performance metric] improved by [percentage]% under [condition], compared to [control/baseline].`
- `The [mechanism] explanation is supported by [characterization evidence], though [alternative mechanism] cannot be fully excluded based on current data.`
- `The [modifier/material] dosage of [value]% shows the best balance of [target property] and [competing property] under laboratory conditions.`
- `These findings are specific to the tested [material source/test conditions/specimen geometry]; extrapolation to field conditions requires additional validation.`
