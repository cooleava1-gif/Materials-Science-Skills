# Durability and Sustainability Phrase Bank

Use this bank for durability testing, aging, environmental exposure, life cycle
assessment, recycled materials, waste utilization, and sustainability claims
across all material families.

---

## phrase-bank

### moisture and water exposure

- `After [immersion/humidity/fog] exposure for [duration] at [condition], [material] retained [percentage]% of [property], indicating [bounded moisture resistance].`
- `Moisture uptake of [value]% (by mass) at [condition] is consistent with [hydrophilicity/porosity/microcracking], which may explain the [property] reduction.`
- `The moisture-induced degradation should be assessed together with [recovery/retention] after drying, because [reversible/irreversible] damage is more relevant for service.`

### freeze–thaw resistance

- `After [number] freeze–thaw cycles ([temperature range], [standard]), [material] showed [mass loss/strength retention/visual deterioration] of [value], indicating [acceptable/marginal/poor] resistance.`
- `The freeze–thaw resistance is attributed to [air entrainment/pore structure/water absorption control], but the durability under [real climate/field condition] may differ from the accelerated test.`

### thermal aging

- `After [RTFO/PAV/oven aging] at [temperature] for [duration], [material] showed [property change] of [value]%, consistent with [oxidation/volatile loss/crosslinking/chain scission].`
- `The aging index, defined as [ratio/change], increased from [value] to [value], suggesting [hardening/embrittlement/softening] under the tested protocol.`
- `Long-term aging prediction using [method/model] extrapolates [value] years of service equivalence, but the model assumes [constant rate/no maintenance/climate], which limits applicability.`

### UV and photochemical degradation

- `After [duration] UV exposure ([standard/wavelength/intensity]), [material] showed [yellowing/embrittlement/surface cracking/property loss] of [value]%.`
- `The UV degradation mechanism is consistent with [chain scission/crosslinking/photo-oxidation] as indicated by [FTIR carbonyl index/color change/gloss loss], but [depth profile/through-thickness] analysis is needed.`

### chemical resistance

- `After immersion in [acid/alkali/salt/solvent] at [concentration] and [temperature] for [duration], [material] retained [percentage]% of [property].`
- `Chemical attack is indicated by [mass change/surface morphology/compositional change] observed by [SEM/EDS/XRD/FTIR], and the degradation rate is [value] per [time unit].`

### fatigue and cyclic loading

- `After [number] cycles at [stress/strain level] and [R-ratio], [material] retained [percentage]% of [static property], with crack initiation at [location/defect].`
- `The S–N curve shows a fatigue limit of [value] at [cycle count], but the scatter band suggests [high variability/defect sensitivity/size effect].`

### LCA — life cycle assessment

- `The [cradle-to-gate/cradle-to-grave/functional unit] analysis shows [GWP/AP/EP] of [value] [kg CO₂-eq/unit], [percentage]% [lower/higher] than [reference material/system].`
- `The system boundary includes [raw material/transport/manufacturing/use/end-of-life] stages, with [stage] contributing [percentage]% of the total [impact category].`
- `Sensitivity analysis indicates that [parameter] has the largest influence on [impact], varying by [range] when [parameter] changes from [low] to [high].`

### recycled and waste materials

- `Incorporating [percentage]% [recycled/waste] [material] as [replacement/additive] [maintained/reduced/increased] [property] by [value]% relative to [virgin/reference].`
- `The [recycled/waste] content should be reported with [source/processing/quality control] information, because variability in [composition/contamination/particle size] affects reproducibility.`
- `Environmental benefit claims require [LCA/carbon footprint/functional unit] evidence; performance improvement alone does not establish sustainability.`

### carbon footprint

- `The embodied carbon of [material/system] is [value] kg CO₂-eq per [functional unit], representing a [percentage]% reduction relative to [reference].`
- `The carbon reduction is achieved through [material substitution/process optimization/energy source/recycled content], but [transport/use phase/end-of-life] contributions should be included.`
- `Carbon neutrality claims require [offset/CCS/biogenic carbon] accounting that goes beyond the present system boundary.`

### service-life prediction

- `The estimated service life of [material] under [condition] is [value] years, based on [Arrhenius/Williams-Landel-Ferry/empirical] model fitted to [accelerated aging data].`
- `Service-life prediction assumes [constant degradation rate/no maintenance/single stressor], which may not hold under [real climate/combined exposure/variable loading].`

---

## reviewer-red-flags

- Claiming durability improvement from a single accelerated test without discussing field relevance or acceleration factor.
- Reporting retention ratio without the absolute pre- and post-exposure values.
- Using "sustainable," "green," or "eco-friendly" without LCA, functional unit, and comparison boundary.
- Claiming recycled content benefit without source, processing, quality control, and environmental evidence.
- Extrapolating service life from short-term accelerated data without validating the model or assumptions.
- Reporting freeze-thaw resistance without specifying cycle count, temperature range, specimen size, and standard.

## safe-claim-patterns

- `Within the tested [aging/cycle/condition] protocol, [material] retains [percentage]% of [property], which is [higher/comparable/lower] than [reference] under the same condition.`
- `The [durability/aging/degradation] trend is consistent with [mechanism] observed by [characterization], but field validation under [real condition] is recommended.`
- `The [recycled/waste] material shows potential for [application] at [replacement level], subject to [quality control/processing optimization/LCA verification].`
- `Sustainability claims are limited to [system boundary/functional unit/impact category] and should not be generalized to [broader context/full life cycle/other regions].`
