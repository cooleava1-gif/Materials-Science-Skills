# Ceramics Phrase Bank

Use this bank for structural ceramics, functional ceramics, bioceramics,
refractories, and advanced ceramic composite manuscripts.

---

## phrase-bank

### sintering

- `Densification of [ceramic] reached [value]% relative density at [temperature]°C with [holding time] h, as measured by [Archimedes/mercury porosimetry/geometry].`
- `The sintering temperature was [reduced/increased] by [value]°C with [sintering aid/dopant/processing route], suggesting [liquid-phase/solid-state/activated] sintering mechanism.`
- `Grain growth beyond [temperature/size] is accompanied by [property degradation/porosity change], indicating a trade-off between densification and microstructural coarsening.`

### mechanical properties

- `The [Vickers/Knoop/indentation] hardness of [ceramic] was [value] GPa at [load/condition], consistent with [dense microstructure/solid-solution hardening/phase assemblage].`
- `Fracture toughness measured by [SENB/SEPB/IF/CNB] was [value] MPa·m^(1/2), with toughening attributed to [crack bridging/deflection/phase transformation/fiber pull-out].`
- `The flexural strength of [ceramic] was [value] MPa ([standard], [support span]), with failure originating from [pore/defect/intergranular fracture] as observed in [SEM fractography].`

### microstructure

- `SEM reveals a [dense/porous/bimodal/elongated] microstructure with grain size [range], which correlates with [mechanical/thermal/electrical] response.`
- `The grain-boundary phase identified as [phase] by [EDS/TEM/EBSD] may contribute to [strength/weakness/conductivity/resistivity], but its volume fraction was not quantified.`
- `Porosity analysis by [MIP/Archimedes/micro-CT] shows [open/closed/interconnected] pores with [size distribution], consistent with [processing/sintering/debinding] conditions.`

### phase identification

- `XRD analysis confirms the presence of [phase A] and [phase B] in [ceramic], with no detectable [secondary phase/residual] within the instrument resolution.`
- `The phase transformation from [α → β / tetragonal → monoclinic] is indicated by [peak shift/splitting/appearance] at [condition], which may affect [mechanical/thermal] behavior.`
- `Rietveld refinement yields [value]% [phase A] and [value]% [phase B], with lattice parameters consistent with [JCPDS/ICDD card number].`

### thermal properties

- `The thermal conductivity of [ceramic] was [value] W m⁻¹ K⁻¹ at [temperature], [higher/lower] than [reference], consistent with [porosity/phase/phonon scattering] contribution.`
- `Thermal expansion measured by [dilatometry] shows a coefficient of [value] × 10⁻⁶ K⁻¹ over [temperature range], with [no anomaly/discontinuity] at [phase transformation temperature].`
- `Thermal shock resistance, assessed by [quench method/gradient test], indicates [critical temperature difference] ΔT for crack initiation.`

### functional properties

- `The [dielectric/piezoelectric/ionic conductivity] of [ceramic] reached [value] at [frequency/temperature], with [low/high] loss tangent of [value].`
- `The [polarization/hysteresis] curve shows [remanent polarization/coercive field] of [value], consistent with [ferroelectric/antiferroelectric/relaxor] behavior.`
- `Impedance spectroscopy reveals [grain/grain-boundary/electrode] contributions, with activation energy of [value] eV for [conduction mechanism].`

### bioceramics

- `The [bioactivity/biocompatibility] of [ceramic] is indicated by [HA formation/cell viability/protein adsorption] after [duration] in [SBF/cell line/animal model].`
- `Degradation rate measured by [mass loss/ion release/pH change] was [value] over [duration], suggesting [resorbable/non-resorbable/controlled degradation] behavior.`
- `The mechanical compatibility with [bone/dental/tissue] is supported by [elastic modulus/hardness/strength] matching, but [long-term resorption/in vivo response] requires further validation.`

---

## reviewer-red-flags

- Reporting sintered density without sintering temperature, atmosphere, heating rate, and holding time.
- Claiming improved toughness without fractographic evidence or toughening mechanism identification.
- Comparing hardness across different loads or indentation methods without normalization.
- Attributing property changes to grain size without quantitative microstructural analysis.
- Claiming phase transformation without temperature-dependent XRD or in situ evidence.
- Reporting functional properties without frequency, temperature, and measurement condition specification.

---

## safe-claim-patterns

- `Within the tested [sintering temperature/composition/dopant] range, [ceramic] achieves [density/property] consistent with [microstructural/phase] evidence.`
- `The [toughness/hardness/strength] improvement is attributed to [mechanism] based on [fractography/XRD/TEM], though contributions from [alternative mechanism] cannot be excluded.`
- `The [thermal/dielectric/ionic] property is promising for [application] at [temperature/frequency/condition], but [long-term stability/cycling/mechanical reliability] remains to be verified.`
