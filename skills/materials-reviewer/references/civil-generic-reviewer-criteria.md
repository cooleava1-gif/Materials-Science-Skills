# Civil Engineering Materials (General) — Reviewer Criteria

## General civil/construction journals

- Clear construction or infrastructure use case.
- Material system, function, and design variables.
- Benchmark or control material.
- Test standards or well-justified custom protocols.
- Performance-to-application link.
- Sustainability claims with functional unit, reference, and boundary.

## Desk-rejection risks

- No construction relevance.
- Generic tests unrelated to service conditions.
- Material characterization without engineering significance.
- Sustainability from waste use alone without performance normalization or LCA.
- Missing control group or benchmark.
