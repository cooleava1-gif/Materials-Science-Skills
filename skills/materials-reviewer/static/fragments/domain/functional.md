# Functional / Electronic Materials — Reviewer Guide

## Key review axes

- **Measurement conditions**: Are temperature, frequency, illumination, and scan rate reported?
- **Device statistics**: Is device-to-device variation reported? Or only the best device?
- **Stability data**: Is the test duration and retention percentage reported?
- **Benchmark comparison**: Are results compared with state-of-the-art for the same material class?
- **Mechanism claims**: Are defect/interface claims supported by direct characterization (XPS, TEM, EPR)?

## Common fatal flaws

- Efficiency/capacity without device area or number of devices
- Stability data from unrealistically short duration
- No forward/reverse scan for J-V curves
- Attributing all performance to the material without considering contact/interface effects

## Typical reviewer questions

- "How many devices were measured? What is the efficiency distribution?"
- "What is the stability test duration and the retention percentage?"
- "How does this performance compare with the current state-of-the-art?"
- "Was the measurement temperature controlled and reported?"
