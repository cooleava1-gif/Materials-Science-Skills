# Journal: Advanced Materials

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/15214095
- https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/15214095/homepage/forauthors.html

## Best fit

Novel materials with broad impact, including new synthesis routes, unique material properties, and applications across energy, electronics, biomedicine, and structural domains. Emphasis on materials innovation with wide relevance.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Novel material composition, structure, or synthesis method.
- Clear demonstration of unique properties or performance.
- Broad impact beyond a single application.
- Graphical abstract effectively conveys the key message.
- Strong structure-property-application relationship.

## Weak fit

- Incremental variation on known materials or methods.
- Narrow application without materials innovation.
- Pure computational study without experimental validation.
- Engineering optimization without new materials insight.
- Manuscript lacks graphical abstract or visual quality is poor.

## Advanced Materials writing stance

Lead with the materials innovation. Novelty should be framed as a materials design or synthesis breakthrough. Use clear, accessible language suitable for a broad materials science audience. Graphical abstract is critical for first impression.

## Review caution

Reviews in Advanced Materials are typically invited or pre-approved. Unsolicited reviews face higher rejection risk. Check current editorial policy before submission.
