# Journal: Advanced Functional Materials

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/16163028
- https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/page/journal/16163028/homepage/forauthors.html

## Best fit

Functional materials with clear application potential, including energy materials, electronic materials, catalytic materials, responsive materials, and materials with demonstrated device-level performance.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Clear functionality demonstrated at material or device level.
- Performance metrics are quantified and compared to benchmarks.
- Structure-function relationship is well-established.
- Application relevance is explicit and compelling.
- Graphical abstract clearly communicates the functional material concept.

## Weak fit

- Material studied without demonstrated functionality.
- Property measured but not linked to application or device.
- Incremental performance improvement over known functional materials.
- Pure fundamental study without application context.
- Missing performance benchmarks or state-of-the-art comparison.

## Advanced Functional Materials writing stance

Frame the work around material functionality and application. Novelty should be expressed as a functional material design or performance breakthrough. Always connect material properties to application performance. Graphical abstract is essential.

## Review caution

Reviews in Advanced Functional Materials are typically invited or pre-approved. Unsolicited reviews face higher rejection risk. Check current editorial policy before submission.
