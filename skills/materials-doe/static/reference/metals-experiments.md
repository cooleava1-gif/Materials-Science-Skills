# Metals Experiment Design Reference

## Typical factors
- Alloy composition (wt% of key elements).
- Heat treatment: temperature, time, cooling rate.
- Thermomechanical processing: deformation ratio, temperature, rate.
- Additive manufacturing: laser power, scan speed, layer thickness.

## Typical responses
- Mechanical: yield strength, UTS, elongation, hardness, impact energy.
- Microstructural: grain size, phase fraction, precipitate size/distribution.
- Environmental: corrosion rate, fatigue life, creep strain.

## Recommended design
- Full or fractional factorial for screening.
- Response surface methodology (RSM) for process optimization.
- Taguchi for robust parameter design.
