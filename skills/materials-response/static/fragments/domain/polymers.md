# Reviewer Response — Domain Context: Polymers

When responding to polymer reviewer comments:
- Thermal characterization (Tg, Tm, Td) is commonly questioned — confirm DSC/TGA details.
- Crosslinking claims are often challenged — provide gel content or spectroscopic evidence.
- Mechanical trade-offs (strength vs. elongation) are frequently raised — present both.
- Aging/degradation test conditions and duration are scrutinized — specify parameters.

Provide evidence-based responses with clear references to manuscript sections.
