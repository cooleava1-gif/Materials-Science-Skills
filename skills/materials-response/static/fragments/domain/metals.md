# Reviewer Response — Domain Context: Metals

When responding to metals reviewer comments:
- Heat treatment parameters (especially cooling rate) are commonly questioned.
- Microstructure quantification (grain size, phase fraction) is often challenged.
- Strength-ductility trade-off without both metrics reported is a frequent weakness.
- Fracture mechanism without fractography is commonly flagged.
- Specimen orientation relative to processing direction is often unclear.

Provide specific data and standard references for each response.
