# Polymers — Figure Guide

## Common figure types

| Figure type | When to use | Key requirements |
|---|---|---|
| DSC thermogram overlay | Comparing thermal transitions (Tg, Tm, Tc) | Heating/cooling rate; endo up/down convention; 2nd cycle preferred |
| TGA/DTG curve | Thermal stability, decomposition stages | Inert/oxidative atmosphere; onset & peak degradation T |
| Stress-strain curve | Mechanical properties | Full curve not just summary; report both strength and elongation |
| DMA plot (E', E", tan δ) | Viscoelastic properties across T/frequency | Heating rate, frequency; indicate Tg from tan δ peak |
| SEM cross-section | Composite dispersion, interface quality | Scale bar; control vs modified; indicate filler/matrix boundary |
| Rheology curve (viscosity, G'/G") | Processing behavior, cure kinetics | Temperature/frequency; log scale; indicate key transitions |

## Typical multi-panel structure

**Panel A**: Thermal characterization (DSC + TGA showing thermal properties)
**Panel B**: Mechanical (stress-strain curves at different formulations)
**Panel C**: Morphology (SEM showing dispersion or fracture surface)
**Panel D**: Application property (barrier, thermal, or aging performance)

## Reviewer-sensitive pitfalls

- ⚠️ DSC/TGA without heating rate or atmosphere specification.
- ⚠️ "Improved mechanical properties" without stress-strain curves (just bar charts).
- ⚠️ DMA without specifying frequency or heating rate.
- ⚠️ SEM at single magnification or without comparison with control.
- ⚠️ Missing error bars on mechanical data.

## Caption boundary phrases

- `DSC thermograms of [polymer] showing Tg/Tm at [value]. Second heating cycle at [rate]°C/min.`
- `TGA/DTG curves under N₂/air atmosphere. Degradation onset at [value]°C with [value]% mass loss at [T].`
- `Representative stress-strain curves of [formulation] at [condition].`
- `SEM micrographs of fracture surface: (a) [neat polymer], (b) [composite].`
