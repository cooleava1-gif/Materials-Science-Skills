# Caption Boundary

Use this contract when drafting captions for multi-panel materials figures.

## Rule

Each panel claim must not exceed the evidence shown in that panel or the
storyboard evidence field.

## Panel claim pattern

- `Panel [A] shows [observable result] under [condition].`
- `Panel [B] supports the interpretation that [mechanism] is associated with [property], based on [evidence].`
- `Panel [C] compares [metric] after [aging/service protocol], so the claim is limited to [protocol boundary].`

## Boundary checks

- Do not claim causality from a property-performance panel alone.
- Do not claim service durability without an aging, moisture, UV, fatigue, fire,
  or field panel.
- Do not turn a mechanism schematic into evidence unless the characterization
  panel is present.
- Do not call a dosage window optimal unless all scoring metrics and weights are
  listed.
