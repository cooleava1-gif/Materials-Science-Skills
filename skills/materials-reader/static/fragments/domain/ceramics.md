# Reader Note — Domain Context

Loaded automatically when the domain is ceramic materials.

When reading a ceramics paper, pay special attention to:
- Powder characteristics (purity, particle size, source).
- Full sintering profile (temperature, ramp, dwell, atmosphere).
- Density measurement method and relative density value.
- Phase identification by XRD (reference patterns, Rietveld).
- Mechanical test standards and Weibull statistics.

Record the sintering conditions, density, phases, and key mechanical/functional properties.
