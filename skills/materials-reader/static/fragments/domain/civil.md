# Reader Note — Domain Context

Loaded automatically when the domain is civil/construction materials.

When reading a civil/construction materials paper, pay special attention to:
- Engineering context and service conditions (traffic, climate, exposure).
- Standard test methods cited (ASTM, EN, JTG, GB/T).
- Dosage range and the rationale for the chosen levels.
- Performance trade-offs (strength vs. workability, adhesion vs. stability).
- Mechanism evidence: at least one of FTIR, SEM, thermal analysis.

Record the material system, modifier details, test conditions, and key quantitative results in the reader package.
