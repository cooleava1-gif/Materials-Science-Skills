# Reader Note — Domain Context

Loaded automatically when the domain is polymer materials.

When reading a polymer paper, pay special attention to:
- Polymer type, grade, molecular weight, and supplier.
- Full processing parameters (temperature, pressure, cooling rate).
- Thermal characterization (DSC with heating rate and cycle number).
- Mechanical properties: both strength and elongation reported.
- Composite details: filler type, loading, surface treatment.

Record the formulation, processing, key transitions (Tg, Tm, Td), and mechanical trade-offs.
