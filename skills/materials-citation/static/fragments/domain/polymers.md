# Polymers Domain Citation Guide

For polymer manuscripts, keep evidence separated by:

- **Polymer type**: thermoplastic vs. thermoset, amorphous vs. semi-crystalline, molecular weight.
- **Thermal properties**: Tg, Tm, Tc, Td from DSC/TGA; processing temperature window.
- **Mechanical properties**: tensile, flexural, impact, DMA (storage modulus, tan δ).
- **Rheological properties**: viscosity, shear thinning, gel point, melt flow index.
- **Composite-specific**: fiber/matrix interface, filler dispersion, aspect ratio, loading.
- **Aging/degradation**: UV, thermal, hydrolytic, and biodegradation evidence.

Separate structure-property citations from mechanism citations.
