# Journal of Materials Chemistry A Submission Guidance

> Verify the current RSC author instructions before submission.

## Submission routes

- Article, Communication, Review, and Perspective.
- Abstracts are unstructured. Use the current article-type limits.

## Required package items

- No Elsevier-style highlights.
- Electronic Supplementary Information when applicable.
- TOC entry at revision.
- Data availability, author-contributions, conflict-of-interest, and
  conditional ethics statements.

## Formatting

- Use the official RSC article template and sequential numbered references.
- Verify figure requirements, APC, portal, and article-type limits before
  submission.
