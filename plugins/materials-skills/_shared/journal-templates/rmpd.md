# RMPD Submission Guidance

> Always verify against the current Guide for Authors before submission.
> Official page: https://www.tandfonline.com/action/authorSubmission?journalCode=trmp20&page=instructions

## Official class

Use Taylor & Francis `interact.cls`. Paste this line:

```latex
\documentclass[interact]{interact}
```

Download the official template from the Taylor & Francis author services
page.

## Metadata fill steps

1. Title, authors, affiliations, corresponding author ORCID and email.
2. Unstructured abstract (~250 words).
3. Keywords (3-6).
4. Mandatory declarations: data availability, CRediT, conflict of interest,
   funding, ethics if applicable.

## Layout notes

- RMPD does not require Elsevier-style highlights.
- References follow Taylor & Francis numeric style.
- Panel labels lowercase in parentheses.

## Submission checklist (assembled from journal-formats facts)

- [ ] Unstructured abstract within word limit
- [ ] Keywords 3-6
- [ ] Data availability statement
- [ ] CRediT author statement
- [ ] Declaration of competing interest
- [ ] Funding statement
- [ ] Ethics statement (if applicable)
- [ ] Figures meet resolution and format requirements
- [ ] References follow Taylor & Francis numeric style
- [ ] Cover letter states finding, novelty, journal fit

## Live-verification placeholders

Before final submission, re-check: word limit, abstract word count,
reference cap, APC rate, submission portal URL.
