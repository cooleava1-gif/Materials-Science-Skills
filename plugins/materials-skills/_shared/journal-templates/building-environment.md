# Building and Environment Submission Guidance

> Verify the current Guide for Authors before submission.

## Submission routes

- Research Article: unstructured 250-word abstract and up to 9,000 words
  excluding references.
- Review Article: verify the current limit.
- Letter: 350-1,000 words and no abstract.
- Comment and Opinion: 800-2,500 words with a 100-word abstract.

Do not use a case-study route for this journal.

## Required package items

- Highlights.
- Graphical abstract (recommended).
- Data availability, CRediT, competing-interest, funding, and conditional
  ethics statements.

## Formatting

- Use the official Elsevier template and numbered references.
- Verify figure requirements, APC, portal, and article-type limits before
  submission.
