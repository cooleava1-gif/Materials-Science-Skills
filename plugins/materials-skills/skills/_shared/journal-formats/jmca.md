# Journal of Materials Chemistry A Formatting Facts

> Verify the current RSC author instructions before final submission.

## Article routes

- Article, Communication, Review, and Perspective.
- Abstracts are unstructured; use current article-type limits.

## Required items

- No Elsevier-style highlights.
- Electronic Supplementary Information when applicable.
- TOC entry at revision.
- Data availability, author contributions, conflicts of interest, and
  conditional ethics statements.
