# Acta Materialia Formatting Facts

> Verify the current Guide for Authors before final submission.

## Article routes

- Research article: unstructured abstract, 250 words.
- Technical Note: verify current length and abstract requirements.
- Standard reviews are commissioned; they are not a general submission route.

## Required items

- Graphical abstract.
- Data availability, CRediT, competing-interest, funding, and conditional
  ethics statements.

Use numbered Elsevier references. The current guide remains authoritative for
figure specifications, portal details, and any article-type limit.
