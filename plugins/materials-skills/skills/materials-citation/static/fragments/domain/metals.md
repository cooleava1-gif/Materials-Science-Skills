# Metals and Alloys Domain Citation Guide

For metals manuscripts, keep evidence separated by:

- **Alloy class**: composition (wt%), grade designation, standard specification.
- **Processing history**: casting, rolling, forging, heat treatment, additive manufacturing parameters.
- **Microstructure**: grain size, phase fraction, precipitate characteristics, texture.
- **Mechanical properties**: strength (yield/UTS), ductility, hardness, toughness, fatigue.
- **Environmental performance**: corrosion (type, standard), hydrogen embrittlement, oxidation.
- **Application-specific**: weldability, formability, high-temperature creep, wear resistance.

Separate processing-microstructure citations from property citations.
