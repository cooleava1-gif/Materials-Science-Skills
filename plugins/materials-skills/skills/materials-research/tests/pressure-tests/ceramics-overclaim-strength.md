# Pressure test: Ceramics — claiming high strength without Weibull statistics

## Theme

overclaim

## Modules Covered

- materials-research
- materials-figure
- materials-data

## Prompt
Draft a Results section for an alumina ceramic paper. The flexural strength data are: 380, 420, 350, 395, 410 MPa (n=5). Write the results.

## Expected Behavior
- Report mean and range, not Weibull modulus.
- Note that 5 specimens are insufficient for Weibull analysis (minimum 30 recommended).
- Do not claim a Weibull modulus value.
- Use cautious language about strength variability.

## Failure Signs
- Inventing a Weibull modulus from 5 specimens.
- Claiming "high reliability" without statistical evidence.
- Reporting p-values or significance for the single-group data.
