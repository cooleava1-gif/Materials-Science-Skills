# Thermal Insulation Characterization and Standards Guide

## Thermal Conductivity Measurement

| Method | Standard | Range | Specimen | Key parameters |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Guarded hot plate | ASTM C177, ISO 8302 | 0.01-1 W/(m.K) | 300x300 mm panels | Mean temp, gradient, heat flow direction |
| Heat flow meter | ASTM C518, ISO 8301 | 0.01-0.5 W/(m.K) | 300x300 mm panels | Mean temp, temp diff, calibration |
| Transient plane source | ISO 22007-2 | 0.01-500 W/(m.K) | 20-50 mm samples | Sensor size, measurement time |
| Laser flash | ASTM E1461 | 0.1-2000 W/(m.K) | 12.7 mm disc | Temperature, diffusivity, specific heat |

## Reporting Requirements

- Standard used and deviations.
- Mean temperature (conductivity is temperature-dependent).
- Specimen conditioning (T, RH, duration).
- Density (bulk density affects conductivity).
- For anisotropic materials: heat flow direction relative to orientation.

## Mechanical Testing

| Property | Standard | Key parameters |
|---|---|---|
| Compression (foams, aerogels) | ASTM C165, ISO 844 | Strain at 10%/25% deformation, loading rate |
| Flexural (boards, panels) | ASTM C203, ISO 12089 | Span, width, loading rate |
| Tensile | ASTM C297, ISO 1926 | Bonding method, loading rate |

## Durability

| Test | Standard | Key parameters |
|---|---|---|
| Moisture absorption | ASTM C272, ISO 12571 | RH conditions, time, equilibrium MC |
| Freeze-thaw | ASTM C666, ISO 20392 | Cycles, property retention |
| Hygrothermal aging | ISO 12579 | T, RH, duration, conductivity change |

## Reporting Rules

- Thermal conductivity meaningless without mean temperature.
- Report density alongside thermal conductivity.
- State moisture content during measurement.
- Cite test standard number and year.
