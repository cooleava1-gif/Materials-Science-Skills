# Domain: Polymers Family

Use for thermoplastics, thermosets, rubber, polymer composites, polymer blends, adhesives, coatings, and polymer-based functional materials. Covers synthesis, processing, characterization, and performance of polymeric systems.

## Core logic

Polymer papers need to connect:

1. Synthesis or formulation design.
2. Processing method and conditions (temperature, pressure, curing, extrusion, injection molding).
3. Structure-property relationship (crystallinity, crosslink density, morphology).
4. Thermal and mechanical performance.
5. Aging, degradation, and long-term stability.

## Key evidence categories

- Chemical structure: FTIR, Raman, NMR, XPS.
- Thermal behavior: DSC, TGA, DMA, HDT, Vicat.
- Mechanical: tensile, impact (Charpy/Izod), flexural, hardness, creep.
- Rheology: viscosity, shear/extensional flow, gel point, viscoelastic moduli.
- Morphology: SEM, TEM, AFM, optical microscopy, SAXS/WAXS.
- Aging: UV exposure, thermal aging, hydrolysis, oxidation induction time.

## Reviewer risks

- Processing-structure gap: processing parameters not connected to observed morphology.
- Missing aging data: only pristine properties reported; no degradation pathway examined.
- Overclaiming from neat resin to composite: filler effects attributed to polymer alone.
- Thermal history ignored: DSC or DMA results depend on thermal protocol but conditions not reported.
- Single mechanical test: tensile only; no impact, fatigue, or creep validation.
- Crosslink density or molecular weight not measured but mechanism relies on it.
