# Domain: Timber, Masonry, and Bamboo Materials

Use for engineered wood, bamboo composites, masonry units, mortar, and natural bio-based building materials.

## Core logic

A timber/masonry/bamboo manuscript must define:

- Material species, grade, or unit type.
- Moisture content and conditioning state.
- Loading direction (parallel/perpendicular to grain for timber/bamboo).
- Adhesive or mortar system.
- Durability exposure (humidity, fungi, insects, fire, weathering).

## Evidence contract

- Density and moisture content (ASTM D4442).
- Compression, flexure, or tension per grain direction (ASTM D143 / ISO 13061).
- Adhesive/mortar bond strength and compatibility.
- Long-term durability under relevant exposure.

## Reviewer risks

- Claiming structural equivalence to conventional materials without normalized properties.
- Ignoring moisture-induced dimensional stability.
- Fire resistance claims without char rate or residual capacity data.
- Durability claims without biological or weathering exposure evidence.
