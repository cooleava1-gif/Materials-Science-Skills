# Journal: Progress in Polymer Science

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/progress-in-polymer-science
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/progress-in-polymer-science/publish/guide-for-authors

## Best fit

Comprehensive review articles covering the full breadth of polymer science, including polymer synthesis, polymer physics, polymer characterization, functional polymers, biopolymers, and polymer applications in energy, biomedicine, and sustainability.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Comprehensive coverage of a polymer science topic.
- Critical analysis and synthesis, not just literature summary.
- Clear identification of future directions and open challenges.
- Written by recognized experts in the field.
- Topic fills a gap — no recent comprehensive review exists on the same scope.

## Weak fit

- Unsolicited submission without editor pre-approval or invitation.
- Topic already covered by a recent comprehensive review.
- Narrow scope insufficient for a review journal.
- Literature summary without critical analysis or synthesis.
- Outdated coverage missing recent key developments.

## Progress in Polymer Science writing stance

Write authoritatively with critical perspective. Synthesize the field rather than merely summarizing individual studies. Identify trends, contradictions, and future opportunities. The audience expects depth and breadth.

## Review caution

Progress in Polymer Science primarily publishes invited reviews. Propose a review outline to the editor before submission. Demonstrate comprehensive expertise and show that the topic fills a genuine gap in the review literature.
