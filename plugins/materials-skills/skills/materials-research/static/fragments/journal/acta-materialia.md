# Journal: Acta Materialia

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/acta-materialia
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/acta-materialia/publish/guide-for-authors

## Best fit

Fundamental materials science with emphasis on processing-structure-property relationships, including metallic materials, structural materials, advanced alloys, ceramics, composites, and materials processing science.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Clear processing-structure-property relationship established.
- Metallic, structural, or advanced materials at the core.
- Comprehensive microstructural characterization provided.
- Quantitative property measurements linked to processing parameters.
- Mechanistic understanding of material behavior demonstrated.

## Weak fit

- Pure computational study without experimental validation.
- Materials study without processing-structure-property connection.
- Single-property measurement without microstructural context.
- Engineering design without materials science contribution.
- Soft matter or biological materials without structural relevance.

## Acta Materialia writing stance

Frame the work around the processing-structure-property triangle. Novelty should be a materials science understanding, not just a new composition or processing route. Quantify the link between processing, microstructure, and properties explicitly.

## Review caution

Acta Materialia publishes reviews but they require invitation or editor pre-approval. Fast Track submissions are for urgent, high-impact findings requiring rapid publication. Check current policy for Fast Track eligibility.
