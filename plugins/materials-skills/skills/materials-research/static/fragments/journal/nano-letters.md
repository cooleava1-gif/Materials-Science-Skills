# Journal: Nano Letters

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://pubs.acs.org/journal/nalefd
- https://pubs.acs.org/page/nalefd/submission/authors.html

## Best fit

Rapid communication of fundamental nanoscience discoveries, including novel nanoscale phenomena, new nanostructure synthesis, nanoscale property measurements, and breakthrough nanoscale characterization.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Fundamental nanoscience discovery with high impact.
- Rapid, concise communication of a key finding.
- Strong nanoscale characterization and quantification.
- Clear novelty — not incremental on prior nanostructures.
- Finding is significant enough for a high-impact Letter format.

## Weak fit

- Applied nanotechnology without fundamental insight.
- Incremental synthesis optimization of known nanostructures.
- Engineering application without nanoscale mechanism.
- Work better suited for a full-length article format.
- Insufficient novelty for a rapid-communication journal.

## Nano Letters writing stance

Be concise and direct. Lead with the fundamental discovery. Every sentence should advance the argument for the finding's significance. Supporting Information should contain detailed methods and supplementary data.

## Review caution

Nano Letters is exclusively a Letter-format journal. Manuscripts must be concise and high-impact. Longer, more comprehensive studies may be better suited for ACS Nano or Journal of the American Chemical Society.
