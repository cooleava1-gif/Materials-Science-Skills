# Polymers Language

Use polymer-specific terminology:

- crosslink density, not "bond density",
- crystallinity (DSC or XRD), not "crystal content",
- degree of cure or gel content, not "hardening",
- melt viscosity or MFI, not "flow",
- molecular weight distribution or PDI, not "molecular size",
- filler dispersion, not "filler mixing",
- interface adhesion or interfacial shear strength, not "filler bonding",
- chain architecture (branching, star, linear), not "molecular shape",
- viscoelastic moduli (G', G''), not "viscosity curves",
- degradation onset or thermal stability, not "heat resistance",
- elongation at break, not "stretchability",
- impact strength (Izod/Charpy), not "toughness" without qualifier.

Do not describe polymer composites only as "reinforced" without reporting filler dispersion evidence (SEM/TEM) and interface characterization.
