# Polymers — Polishing Guide

## Terminology to verify

| Term | Domain-specific check |
|---|---|
| Tg / Tm / Tc | Must identify transition correctly; specify DSC heating rate and cycle |
| crosslinking | Requires gel content, DSC exotherm, or FTIR evidence — not inferred from performance |
| compatibility | Blend compatibility requires single Tg (DSC/DMA), not mechanical data alone |
| molecular weight | Mn, Mw, or PDI? Report method (GPC, viscometry) |
| degradation | TGA onset temperature or isothermal? Specify atmosphere (N₂ vs air) |

## Common overclaim patterns

| Overclaim | Safer alternative |
|---|---|
| "The composite exhibits superior mechanical properties" | "The composite showed X% higher tensile strength than the neat polymer" |
| "Complete crosslinking was achieved" | "The gel content reached X%, suggesting a high degree of crosslinking" |
| "The polymer is biodegradable" | "X% mass loss was observed after Y days in [standard] composting conditions" |

## Hedging guide

| Evidence level | Verb | Example |
|---|---|---|
| DSC/DMA with multiple samples | confirms, demonstrates | "DMA confirms a single Tg, indicating a miscible blend" |
| Single technique, no replicates | suggests, is consistent with | "SEM suggests good filler dispersion" |
| Mechanical data only | may indicate, could be due to | "The increased modulus may indicate improved crosslinking" |

## Journal-specific language

- Prefer precise thermal notation: "T_g = 85°C" not "glass transition at 85°C".
- Report PDI for molecular weight. "Narrow distribution" requires PDI < 1.2.
- For creep: specify temperature, stress level, and duration.
