# Polishing Contract

> **Domain context**: The `domain` axis has loaded domain-specific polishing guidance for [detected domain]. The contract below is universal; the domain guide contains domain-specific terminology checks, overclaim patterns, and hedging standards.

Preserve:

- data,
- units,
- test conditions,
- citation intent,
- uncertainty,
- limitations,
- author meaning.

Improve:

- sentence clarity,
- paragraph flow,
- evidence-weighted verbs,
- journal-appropriate tone,
- terminology consistency.

Flag rather than silently fix:

- unsupported novelty,
- missing statistical evidence,
- vague mechanism claims,
- sustainability claims without boundary,
- "significant" without statistical support.
