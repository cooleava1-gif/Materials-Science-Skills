# Algorithm / Software Inventions

Identify inputs, processing steps, intermediate data, and outputs. Prefer
observable operations over labels such as "intelligent module" or "novel
network."

## Special handling

- Core formulas must be retained with editable Office Math in DOCX.
- Every formula symbol must be defined.
- Technical role of each formula must be explained.

## Subject-matter gate

- A pure algorithm/software prediction claim is an Article 25 subject-matter
  risk under Chinese patent practice; do not present model accuracy alone as a
  patentable technical contribution.
- Before formal claims, identify a concrete technical feature: a disclosed
  manufacturing or processing step, sensor/controller loop, apparatus, or
  other measurable technical effect. If none is supplied, block the claim and
  mark the missing feature `needs-confirmation`.
- A quality-control or concrete technical process reframing is only a bounded
  suggestion. Do not add sensors, actuators, manufacturing steps, or technical
  effects that the source does not disclose.
