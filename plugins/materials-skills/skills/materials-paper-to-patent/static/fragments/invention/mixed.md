# Mixed Inventions

Use when the source discloses more than one of process / apparatus /
algorithm and no single category is dominant.

## Strategy

- Draft at least one product claim (apparatus) and at least one method
  claim (process), each as a separate independent claim category.
- Do not multiply categories reflexively — only add what the disclosure
  supports.
- Keep dependent claim chains within a single category; do not mix
  apparatus and process dependencies.
