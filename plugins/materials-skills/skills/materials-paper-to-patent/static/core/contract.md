# Contract

`materials-paper-to-patent` converts source-grounded materials research into a
Chinese invention-patent draft package. Every claim feature must map to a
source identifier, and unsupported technical effects remain marked for author
input.

The skill may produce `draft.json`, claim audits, DOCX package files, and
flowchart notes. It must not invent experimental evidence, legal conclusions,
inventor identities, filing dates, priority claims, or official CNIPA outcomes.
