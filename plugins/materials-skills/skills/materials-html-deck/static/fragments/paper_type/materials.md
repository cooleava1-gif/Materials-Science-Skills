# Paper type: materials / experimental / engineering

Covers experimental materials-science papers whose core contribution is a new material, formulation, processing route, or performance improvement: composites, coatings, asphalt/cement/pavement materials, polymers, metals, ceramics, functional materials, nanomaterials, and related engineering applications.

## Presentation arc — property-to-mechanism / design-to-performance

Best when the paper designs or modifies a material and must show that the change produces a measurable, explainable benefit. Order the story as:

1. the engineering or scientific problem,
2. the material design strategy (formulation, processing, modification),
3. the material/ microstructure/ characterization evidence,
4. the performance test and key results,
5. the mechanism linking design to performance,
6. validation, standards, or field relevance,
7. limitations and next steps.

## Default slide structure (adapt, do not force)

1. 标题页
2. 工程/科学背景：问题与需求
3. 现有材料的瓶颈或知识缺口
4. 材料设计策略 / 改性思路
5. 制备 / 配比 / 工艺路线
6-7. 表征证据（微观结构、组分、界面等）
8-9. 性能结果（力学、耐久、粘结、功能性等）
10. 机理分析 / 结构-性能关系
11. 验证、标准测试或工程适用性
12. 局限性与未解决问题
13. 总结与展望

Emphasise the design-to-performance link: each result slide should connect a material choice to a measured outcome or a mechanistic explanation. Use performance curves, bar charts, or standards tables as hero evidence; keep microstructure images in a supporting role unless they are the main finding.
