# Evaluation Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 10. Use these anchors: 3 is weak, 5 is a
usable internal draft, 7 is supervisor-facing, and 9 is near submission polish.
Route the next revision by the lowest score.

## Dimensions

- `task_scope_clarity`: scope, audience, deliverable, and blocked claims.
- `scientific_tension`: field progress, unresolved gap, and material consequence.
- `evidence_match`: major claims mapped to evidence with honest strength labels.
- `materials_method_reproducibility`: materials, preparation, standards, conditions,
  replicates, and statistics.
- `argument_chain`: paragraph messages support the one-sentence argument in order.
- `innovation_specificity`: contribution is specific, bounded, and distinguished.
- `risk_boundary`: mechanism, durability, application, citation, and comparator risks.
- `language_quality`: clear terminology, focused paragraphs, and readable prose.

## Thresholds

| Use case | Minimum average | Blocking rule |
|---|---:|---|
| Foundation readiness | 7.5 | No dimension below 6.5 |
| Section draft | 6.5 | Evidence match and risk boundary at least 6.0 |
| Manuscript or proposal review | 7.0 | No unsupported major claim |
| Final polish handoff | 8.0 | No dimension below 7.0 |
