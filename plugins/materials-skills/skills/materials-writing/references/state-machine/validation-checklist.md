# Validation Checklist

Run this checklist after Gate 2 content review and before Gate 4 scoring.

## Traceability

- Every major factual claim maps to the evidence table or an explicit placeholder.
- Evidence status is not inflated to compensate for missing source depth.
- Unsupported claims do not appear as unqualified manuscript claims.

## Materials And Methods

- Material identifiers, dosages, preparation, conditioning, standards, replicates,
  statistics, and uncertainty are visible or marked missing.

## Argument And Risk

- The argument map matches the draft.
- The target section's allowed and forbidden claims are defined.
- Mechanism claims have direct characterization or are softened as inference.
- Durability, field-transfer, and alternative-explanation boundaries are stated.

## Output

- The response includes current artifact, score/status, remaining risks,
  stop-or-continue reason, and one next action.
- `state.json` is updated or a state patch is provided when direct editing is
  not possible.
