# Validation Checklist

Use this checklist after Gate 2 content review and before Gate 4 score
threshold decisions.

## Traceability
- Every major factual claim maps to `02_evidence_table.md`.
- Evidence status is not inflated to compensate for missing source depth.
- Claims marked `unsupported` do not appear as manuscript claims.

## Materials Methods
- Material identifiers, dosages, preparation route, and conditioning are stated.
- Test standards or protocol families are named when relevant.
- Replicate count, statistics, and uncertainty are visible or marked missing.

## Argument And Sections
- The one-sentence argument in `03_argument_map.md` matches the draft.
- Section contracts define allowed and forbidden claims for the target section.
- Paragraphs carry one message and are easy to reverse-outline.

## Risk Boundary
- Mechanism claims have characterization or are softened as inference.
- Durability, aging, application, or field-transfer boundaries are stated.
- Rival explanations are acknowledged when the evidence invites them.

## Output
- The response includes current artifact, score/status, remaining risks,
  stop-or-continue reason, and next action.
- `state.json` is updated or a state patch is provided when direct file update
  is not possible.
