# Nanomaterials Phrase Bank

Use this bank for nanoparticles, 2D materials, thin films, nanocomposites, and quantum dot manuscripts.

---

## phrase-bank

### synthesis

- `[Material] nanoparticles were synthesized via [method] using [precursor] as precursor and [capping agent/surfactant/template] to control [size/morphology/dispersity].`
- `The [hydrothermal/solvothermal/sol-gel/co-precipitation/CVD] synthesis yielded [morphology: spherical/rod/plate/flower] particles with an average [diameter/length/thickness] of [value] nm, as determined from TEM.`
- `The [exfoliation/intercalation] method produced [few-layer/monolayer/multilayer] [material] with lateral dimensions of [value] µm, confirmed by [AFM/Raman/TEM].`

### size and morphology

- `TEM analysis reveals [morphology] particles with a mean size of [value] ± [error] nm (n = [number] particles), and a [narrow/broad/unimodal/bimodal] size distribution.`
- `The [hydrodynamic diameter/Z-average] measured by DLS was [value] nm ([PDI value]), [larger/smaller] than the TEM primary size, consistent with [hydration/aggregation/capping layer].`
- `SEM cross-sectional imaging confirms [uniform/non-uniform] dispersion of [nanomaterial] within the [matrix], with [agglomerate size/cluster spacing] of [value].`

### structural characterization

- `XRD patterns show diffraction peaks corresponding to [phase/JCPDS card], with crystallite size of [value] nm calculated from the [hkl] peak using the Scherrer equation.`
- `Raman spectroscopy reveals characteristic [G/D/2D] bands at [value] cm⁻¹, with an [I_D/I_G ratio] of [value] indicating [low/moderate/high] defect density.`
- `The [UV-vis/PL] spectrum shows absorption/emission at [value] nm, corresponding to a bandgap of [value] eV calculated from [Tauc plot/onset].`

### surface chemistry

- `XPS survey scan confirms the presence of [elements], with high-resolution [element] spectra showing [chemical state/bonding configuration] at [binding energy] eV.`
- `Zeta potential measurements show a surface charge of [value] mV at pH [value], indicating [stable/unstable] colloidal dispersion due to [electrostatic/steric] stabilization.`
- `FTIR spectrum shows characteristic peaks at [value] cm⁻¹, attributed to [functional group], confirming [surface modification/ligand attachment/functionalization].`

### property reporting

- `The [catalytic/photocatalytic] activity reached [value] [unit] under [condition], which is [factor] times higher than [bulk/commercial/control] due to [size/surface area/defect] effects.`
- `The [material] exhibits [plasmonic/fluorescent/magnetic] properties with [figure of merit] of [value], showing [dependence/independence] on [size/shape/aspect ratio].`
- `Nanocomposites with [value] wt% [nanomaterial] loading show a [percentage]% improvement in [mechanical/thermal/barrier] property, attributed to [mechanism].`

### stability and dispersion

- `Colloidal stability assessed by [DLS/UV-vis/zeta potential] over [time/duration] shows [no significant aggregation/sedimentation] under [condition].`
- `The [nanomaterial] retains [percentage]% of its initial [property] after [duration] in [medium], suggesting [adequate/poor] stability for [application].`
- `Aggregation observed at [condition/concentration] limits the achievable [property/performance], indicating the need for [better dispersion/surface modification].`

---

## reviewer-red-flags

- Reporting particle size from XRD (Scherrer) alone without TEM confirmation or statistics.
- Claiming "monodisperse" without size distribution histogram (≥100 particles from TEM).
- Overinterpreting hydrodynamic size from DLS as primary particle size.
- Ignoring batch-to-batch variability (single batch is not proof of reproducibility).
- Claiming "green synthesis" without comparative energy/toxicity data.
- Attributing catalytic activity to size effect without surface area normalization.

---

## safe-claim-patterns

- `TEM of [value] particles yields a mean size of [value] nm with [range] distribution, consistent with XRD crystallite size of [value] nm within measurement uncertainty.`
- `The [property] enhancement at [size/morphology] is attributed to [mechanism] based on [evidence type], though contributions from [alternative factor] cannot be ruled out.`
- `Within the tested [concentration/size] range, [nanomaterial] demonstrates [performance] for [application], pending [stability/scalability/toxicity] validation.`
- `DLS shows hydrodynamic size of [value] nm (PDI [value]), suggesting [dispersion state], though TEM remains the reference for primary size determination.`
