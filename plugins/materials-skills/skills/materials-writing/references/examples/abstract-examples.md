# Abstract Examples Index

Materials abstracts live or die on "performance number + condition", not broad
significance. Pick the template whose claim shape matches your evidence.

1. Performance breakthrough — `abstract/template-a-performance-breakthrough.md`
   `Use when one property jumps clearly past a stated ceiling under a stated condition.`
2. Trade-off resolution — `abstract/template-b-tradeoff-resolution.md`
   `Use when your design improves two competing properties at once (e.g. strength-toughness).`
3. Mechanism clarification — `abstract/template-c-mechanism-clarification.md`
   `Use when the contribution is a clarified mechanism, not a new performance record.`
