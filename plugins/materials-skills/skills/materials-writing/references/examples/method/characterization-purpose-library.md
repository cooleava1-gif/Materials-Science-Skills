# Characterization-Purpose Library

`Per-technique templates for the "purpose sentence + parameter report + result interpretation" pattern. Distilled from published-article-patterns Pattern G/J and the domain fragments. Replace [...] with your specifics.`

## FTIR — chemical bonds / functional groups / reaction progress

- Purpose: `FTIR spectra were recorded on [仪器型号] ([范围] cm⁻¹, [扫描次数] scans, [分辨率] cm⁻¹) to monitor [化学键/官能团/反应进程] through the [特征峰位置] cm⁻¹ peak.`
- Result: `The [特征峰] peak [decreased/disappeared/shifted] for [配比] group (Fig. [x]), consistent with [反应/交联/降解].`

## XRD — phase composition / crystallinity / lattice parameters

- Purpose: `XRD patterns were collected on [仪器型号] ([靶材], [电压/电流], [扫描范围/步长]) to identify [相组成/结晶度/晶格参数].`
- Result: `Diffraction peaks at [2θ角度] correspond to [晶面/相], and the [峰宽/强度] change indicates [晶粒尺寸/结晶度/相变] at [条件].`

## SEM — micro-morphology / fracture surface / interface

- Purpose: `SEM imaging was performed on [仪器型号] ([加速电压], [工作距离], [放大倍数]) to observe [微观形貌/断口/界面].`
- Result: `The [配比] group showed [形貌特征] at [放大倍数], indicating [致密化/界面结合/缺陷] compared with the control.`

## TEM — nanostructure / lattice / defects

- Purpose: `TEM was performed on [仪器型号] ([加速电压]) to resolve [纳米结构/晶格条纹/缺陷].`
- Result: `Lattice fringes of [间距] nm were observed, consistent with [晶面/相]; [缺陷特征] indicates [位错/层错/界面].`

## DSC / TGA — thermal transitions / thermal stability

- Purpose: `DSC/TGA was performed on [仪器型号] ([升温速率], [温度范围], [气氛]) to determine [玻璃化转变/熔融/分解/热稳定性].`
- Result: `The [转变温度] shifted from [数值] to [数值] for [配比], indicating [交联度/结晶度/热稳定性] change.`

## Mechanical testing — strength / modulus / toughness

- Purpose: `[强度/模量/韧性] was measured according to [标准号] on [试件几何], with [重复次数] replicates, at [加载速率/条件].`
- Result: `The [配比] group reached [数值] ± [不确定度] [单位], [百分比] higher than the control ([数值]) under [测试条件].`

## Usage

- Every technique entry in your Methods must have a purpose sentence — "X was performed" alone is a red flag.
- The result template uses hedged verbs (`consistent with`, `indicates`) — reserve `confirms` for multi-technique convergence.
- Parameters in brackets are mandatory for reproducibility; keep a placeholder if unknown rather than omitting.
