# Abstract Template C — Mechanism Clarification

`Use when the contribution is a clarified mechanism, not a new performance record. Distilled from abstract-claim-arc.md Move 4 and published-article-patterns Pattern J/O.`

```latex
% Move 1: long-standing debate or unresolved mechanism
Although [性能改善] in [材料家族] is well documented, the underlying [机制争议] remains contested, with [派别A] attributing it to [机制A] and [派别B] to [机制B].

% Move 2: what evidence has been missing
Resolving this debate requires [缺失的关键表征/测试条件], which previous studies have not reported together with [性能数据].

% Move 3: this characterization strategy
Here, we combined [表征技术A] with [表征技术B] on [材料体系] across [变量范围] to distinguish [机制A] from [机制B].

% Move 4: key finding — the discriminating observation
[表征技术A] showed [可观察特征] at [条件/配比], while [表征技术B] ruled out [替代解释] because [证据], supporting [机制A] over [机制B] within [测试范围].

% Move 5: mechanism implication — what this means for design
This mechanism implies that [设计含义], which reframes [性能调控] as a [结构/工艺参数] problem rather than a [旧认识] problem.

% Move 6: what still needs verification
Because [未测量的变量/尺度] was not included, the mechanism is supported as an interpretation rather than a confirmed cause; [后续验证实验] is needed.
```

## Usage

- Move 1 must name the debate and both competing explanations — a mechanism abstract without a contested alternative is just a result abstract.
- Move 4 is the discriminating observation: it must show why one mechanism fits better, not just that a feature exists.
- Move 6 must keep the verb at `supported` or `consistent with`, never `confirmed` or `proven`.

## Anti-overclaim check

- Do NOT write: `[表征] confirms the mechanism of [性能].`
- Write instead: `[表征] supports a mechanism in which [bounded explanation] under the tested [范围].`
- Do NOT drop the alternative explanation in Move 4 — excluding it is the whole point of a mechanism paper.
