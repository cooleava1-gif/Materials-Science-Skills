# Reviewer Response — Domain Context: Civil / Construction

When responding to civil materials reviewer comments:
- Standard numbers (ASTM, GB/T) are often challenged — verify and cite correctly.
- Dosage optimization without full range is a common weakness — explain scope.
- Field applicability without field data is a frequent concern — acknowledge limitation.
- Moisture/durability testing conditions are often questioned — specify parameters.

Provide point-by-point responses with manuscript location and evidence basis.
