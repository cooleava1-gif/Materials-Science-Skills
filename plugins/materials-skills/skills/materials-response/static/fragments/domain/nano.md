# Reviewer Response — Domain Context: Nanomaterials

When responding to nanomaterials reviewer comments:
- Size characterization approach (TEM vs DLS vs XRD) is commonly questioned.
- Particle counting statistics (≥100) are frequently requested.
- Batch-to-batch reproducibility is often raised.
- Colloidal stability evidence is commonly challenged.
- Surface chemistry claims without XPS/FTIR/zeta potential are flagged.

Provide comprehensive characterization data with appropriate statistics.
