# Reviewer Response — Domain Context: Functional Materials

When responding to functional materials reviewer comments:
- Device-to-device variation and statistics are commonly questioned.
- Stability test duration and conditions are frequently challenged.
- Benchmark comparison with state-of-the-art is often requested.
- Measurement conditions (scan rate, temperature, illumination) are scrutinized.
- Active area specification and device architecture details are commonly raised.

Provide complete measurement conditions and statistical data for each response.
