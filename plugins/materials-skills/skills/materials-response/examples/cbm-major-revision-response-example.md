# CBM Major Revision Response Example

## Reviewer Comment

The manuscript needs stronger evidence for the proposed mechanism and should clarify how the material system performs after moisture conditioning.

## Good Response

We thank the reviewer for this helpful comment. We revised the mechanism discussion to distinguish direct observations from interpretation, added the available microstructural evidence to Section [X], and softened unsupported causal language. We also added moisture-conditioned performance data in Figure [Y] and now report retention values with replicate counts in Section [Z].

## Why This Works

The response acknowledges the concern, names concrete manuscript changes, and keeps the mechanism claim within the supplied evidence.
