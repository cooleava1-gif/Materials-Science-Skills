# CCC Methodology Critique Response Example

## Reviewer Comment

The methodology used to characterize the hydration process is insufficient. First, qualitative XRD analysis alone cannot substantiate the claim regarding phase evolution; the authors must perform Rietveld refinement to quantify the crystalline phases (e.g., alite consumption and CH content). Second, relying solely on compressive strength to deduce hydration kinetics is speculative; complementary evidence such as isothermal calorimetry and thermogravimetric analysis (TGA) must be provided.

## Good Response

We thank the reviewer for these rigorous methodological suggestions. The response should distinguish a justified methodological boundary from missing evidence, and should only claim new TGA or calorimetry data when the author/source provides them.

### R2.2: Compressive Strength vs. Hydration Kinetics
**Concern:** Speculative hydration kinetics based solely on compressive strength.
**Response:**
We agree that compressive strength is an indirect physical consequence of hydration rather than a direct measure of chemical reaction kinetics. If isothermal calorimetry or TGA data are available, the response should state the verified test duration, curing ages, and output metrics. If not, the revised manuscript should remove kinetic overclaims and identify the missing direct evidence.

## Why This Works

This response is effective because:
1. It handles a demanding methodology critique by distinguishing what is scientifically reliable (TGA for bound water in blended binders) from what is prone to high experimental error (Rietveld refinement without external standards in amorphous blended systems).
2. It provides a robust, professional disagreement (DISAGREE) based on physical-chemical constraints of the material system (high amorphous hump, peak overlap) rather than excuses of cost or availability.
3. It complements the disagreement with verified alternative evidence when available, or with an explicit limitation when evidence is missing.
4. It includes exact text diffs demonstrating how speculative claims were pruned and replaced with quantitative data.
