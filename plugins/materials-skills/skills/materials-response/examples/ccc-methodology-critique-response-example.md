# CCC Methodology Critique Response Example

## Reviewer Comment

The authors should justify the selected test method and explain whether the number of replicates is sufficient for the statistical comparison.

## Good Response

We agree that the method rationale needed to be clearer. Section [X] now explains why the selected standard test directly addresses the study objective, and Section [Y] reports the replicate count, mean values, standard deviations, and the statistical test used for group comparison. We also revised the conclusion to avoid claiming significance beyond the supported analysis.

## Why This Works

The response repairs the methodological gap without overstating the data and links each claim to a manuscript location.
