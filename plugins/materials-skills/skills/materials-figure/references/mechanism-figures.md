# Mechanism Figures

> **Domain context**: The `domain` axis has loaded domain-specific figure guidance. Mechanism evidence expectations vary by domain — what one domain accepts as proof, another requires additional techniques for.

Use for FTIR, SEM, TEM, fluorescence microscopy, XRD, TGA, DSC, rheology, EBSD, XPS, or mechanism schematics.

Rules:

- Spectra/images do not speak by themselves.
- Annotate only meaningful peaks, phases, interfaces, or morphology features.
- Tie every mechanism panel to a performance trend (not just "here is a spectrum").
- Mention if evidence is qualitative or based on a single technique.

## Domain-specific mechanism standards

- **Civil/Polymers**: FTIR + SEM + thermal (DSC/TGA) as minimum for mechanism claims.
- **Ceramics**: XRD + SEM/TEM + density; EDS for elemental confirmation.
- **Metals**: SEM + EBSD/TEM + mechanical correlation; fractography for fracture mechanism.
- **Functional**: XPS + TEM + XRD + electrical/optical correlation; EPR for defect claims.
- **Nanomaterials**: TEM (statistics) + XRD + UV-vis/PL + surface characterization (XPS or zeta potential).
