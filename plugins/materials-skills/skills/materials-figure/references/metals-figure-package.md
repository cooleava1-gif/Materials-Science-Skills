# Metals Figure Package

Recommended package for metals and alloys characterization:

1. Composition–processing–property overview (alloy grade or heat treatment vs key property),
2. Stress–strain curves for different heat treatment conditions or processing routes,
3. Hardness profile (through-thickness or along weld cross-section),
4. Microstructure comparison: optical/SEM images of different conditions (annealed, quenched, tempered, as-built AM),
5. Corrosion polarization curves or salt spray mass loss comparison,
6. Fatigue S-N curves or crack growth rate (da/dN vs ΔK),
7. Toughness–strength trade-off map (K_IC vs yield strength or Charpy vs hardness).

Caption stance: processing-microstructure-property chain is more important than "higher strength = better". Report specimen orientation (LT, TL, ST) and test conditions alongside property data.
