# Nanomaterials — Reviewer Guide

## Key review axes

- **Size characterization**: Is TEM size distribution reported with statistics (≥100 particles)?
- **Size vs. hydrodynamic size**: Are both TEM (primary) and DLS (hydrodynamic) reported?
- **Surface chemistry**: Are surface modification claims supported by FTIR, XPS, or zeta potential?
- **Aggregation**: Is colloidal stability assessed? Are agglomeration effects addressed?
- **Batch reproducibility**: Are results from multiple batches shown?

## Common fatal flaws

- Particle size from XRD alone (Scherrer) without TEM confirmation
- "Monodisperse" claimed without size distribution histogram
- DLS without PDI or weighting information
- Batch variability not addressed (single batch results)
- "Green synthesis" claimed without comparative analysis

## Typical reviewer questions

- "How many particles were counted for the size distribution?"
- "What is the PDI from DLS? Is the hydrodynamic size consistent with TEM?"
- "Were the results reproduced across multiple synthesis batches?"
- "Is the colloidal stability assessed under relevant conditions?"
