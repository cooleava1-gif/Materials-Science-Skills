# Metals and Alloys — Reviewer Guide

## Key review axes

- **Microstructure characterization**: Is grain size quantified (ASTM E112)? Is phase identification supported?
- **Mechanical testing**: Are both strength and ductility reported? Specimen orientation stated?
- **Fracture analysis**: Is fractography (SEM) provided for fracture mechanism claims?
- **Heat treatment**: Are temperature, time, AND cooling rate all reported?
- **Corrosion/ environmental testing**: Is the test standard and exposure condition specified?

## Common fatal flaws

- Strength reported without ductility (tensile curve not shown)
- Grain refinement claimed without grain size measurement
- Heat treatment without cooling rate (the most critical parameter)
- Fracture mechanism from tensile curve alone (no fractography)

## Typical reviewer questions

- "What is the grain size and how was it measured?"
- "What is the cooling rate after heat treatment?"
- "How does the strength-ductility combination compare with existing grades?"
- "What is the specimen orientation relative to the processing direction?"
