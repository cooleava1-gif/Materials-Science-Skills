# Timber, Masonry, and Bamboo — Reviewer Criteria

## CBM / JBE / Bioresources journals

- Species/grade/source for timber/bamboo; unit type and mortar for masonry.
- Moisture content at testing and conditioning (ASTM D4442).
- Loading direction for anisotropic materials.
- Adhesive/mortar system, mixing ratio, curing.
- Mechanical: compression, flexure, tension, shear per relevant standard.
- Durability: fungal, moisture, fire, or weathering exposure and acceptance criteria.

## Desk-rejection risks

- Structural claims without moisture content or conditioning.
- Anisotropic properties reported without direction.
- Durability claims without relevant exposure evidence.
- Fire performance without charring rate or residual capacity.
