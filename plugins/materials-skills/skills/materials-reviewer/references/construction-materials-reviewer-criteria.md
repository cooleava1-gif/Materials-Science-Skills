# Construction and Building Materials — Reviewer Criteria

## CBM / JBE / Construction journals

- Material function: structural, repair, protective, thermal, acoustic, durability, sustainability.
- Service environment and design life.
- Complete experimental matrix with design variables and controls.
- Comparison with existing/conventional materials.
- Standard test methods or rationale for custom tests.
- Figures that directly support measured performance.

## Desk-rejection risks

- Narrow incremental contribution.
- No control group.
- Missing test standards.
- Unsupported field-performance claims.
- Sustainability claimed from waste inclusion alone.
