# Waterproofing and Sealant Materials — Reviewer Criteria

## CBM / JBE / Membrane journals

- Chemistry and product class (bitumen, PU, silicone, acrylic, etc.).
- Substrate type, surface preparation, and application method.
- Thickness/coverage and curing conditions.
- Tensile/elongation standard and specimen geometry (ASTM D412 / GB/T 328.9).
- Adhesion: pull-off method, substrate, conditioning (ASTM D4541 / GB/T 16777).
- Water resistance: immersion, pressure, or permeability protocol.
- Aging: UV, heat, water immersion, thermal cycling matching service exposure.

## Desk-rejection risks

- Field-ready claims without installation and aging constraints.
- Adhesion without substrate and conditioning details.
- Sealant performance without joint movement data.
- Membrane lifetime from single-factor accelerated test.
