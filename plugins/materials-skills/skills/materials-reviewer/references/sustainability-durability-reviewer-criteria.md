# Sustainability and Durability of Construction Materials — Reviewer Criteria

## CBM / JBE / CCC / LCA journals

- Functional unit and system boundary for LCA/sustainability claims.
- Reference material and performance normalization.
- Replacement level and source of SCMs, recycled, or waste materials.
- Durability exposure: freeze-thaw, carbonation, chloride, sulfate, ASR, UV, thermal cycling.
- Test standards: ISO 14040/14044, ASTM C666, ASTM C1202, ASTM C1012, etc.
- End-of-life or leaching risk when relevant.

## Desk-rejection risks

- "Sustainable" from waste use alone without LCA or performance comparison.
- Durability claims from single test or unrelated exposure.
- Functional unit or system boundary missing.
- No reference material for performance normalization.
