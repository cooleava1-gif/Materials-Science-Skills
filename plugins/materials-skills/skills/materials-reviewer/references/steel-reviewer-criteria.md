# Steel and Metal Structures — Reviewer Criteria

## CBM / JBE / Corrosion journals

- Material grade/alloy and supplier/source.
- Exposure condition: chloride concentration, humidity, temperature, atmospheric class.
- Surface preparation: blasting grade, cleanliness, roughness.
- Coating/adhesion: type, thickness, application method, pull-off adhesion (ASTM D4541).
- Mechanical: tensile testing standard, specimen geometry, loading rate (ASTM E8).
- Corrosion: mass loss, polarization, EIS, or coating degradation metrics.
- Fire or fatigue: exposure protocol and post-exposure property retention.

## Desk-rejection risks

- Corrosion resistance inferred from visual observation only.
- Coating adhesion without surface preparation details.
- Weld or connection performance without procedure/quality data.
- Fire resistance without temperature-time curve and residual capacity.
